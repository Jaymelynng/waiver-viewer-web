Level 1 Star System

Building the Skill

Each star focuses on one important learning target. This helps athletes build the skill step by step.

Final Star

The Final Star means the athlete can perform the full skill correctly 3 times in a row. This shows:

  • The skill is connected
  • The key parts happen together
  • The athlete can perform it with control and consistency

How Stars Build the Skill

The Star System shows:

  • What the athlete is learning now
  • What comes next
  • When the full skill is ready
Level 1 Move-Up Guide: Athletes move through Level 1 by earning stars in each skill. Each star marks progress on an important part of the skill. The Final Star shows the athlete can perform the full skill correctly and consistently.
Vault
Straight Jump to Resi
  • ★ 1 Run accelerates into the hurdle
  • ★ 2 Arms down on the board, up in the air
  • ★ 3 Straight body and legs in the air
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Handstand Flatback
  • ★ 1 Feet come together at vertical handstand
  • ★ 2 Straight line from hands to toes at vertical
  • ★ 3 Lands flat on the mat as one piece
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Bars
Kickover Pullover
  • ★ 1 Chin stays at the bar until toes come over
  • ★ 2 Shifts hands with fingers forward to finish in front support
  • ★ 3 Straight legs throughout the skill
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Cast
  • ★ 1 Straight arms throughout the skill
  • ★ 2 Legs straight and together
  • ★ 3 Demonstrates straight line from shoulders to toes
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Front Support Hold → Roll Down
  • ★ 1 Holds front support with straight arms
  • ★ 2 Chin to chest
  • ★ 3 Legs straight and together, lowers slowly to a hang
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Beam
Beam Mount
  • ★ 1 Jumps to front support with straight arms
  • ★ 2 Straight leg swings over without touching the beam
  • ★ 3 Stands up with arms by ears
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Lever
  • ★ 1 Arms by ears throughout
  • ★ 2 Straight line from fingers to back foot throughout
  • ★ 3 Back leg stays straight
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Relevé Walk
  • ★ 1 Heels stay off the beam the entire walk
  • ★ 2 Walks with straight legs
  • ★ 3 Chest up with arms out to the side
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Straight Jump
  • ★ 1 Arms swing up as feet leave the beam and down to sides on landing

  • ★ 3 Lands in the same spot it started
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
Straight Jump Dismount

  • ★ 3 Sticks landing with hands in front, knees bent
  • ★ FINAL — Puts it all together correctly 3 times in a row
